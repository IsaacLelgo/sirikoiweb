About Us

At Sirikoi Lodge, we are committed to sustainability and community development. Our mission is to protect the environment while enriching the lives of those in our community.

Sustainability Initiatives

Wood Ash Vinegar from Charcoal

We produce wood ash vinegar from leftover charcoal. This initiative reduces waste and provides a natural cooking and cleaning solution.

Reusing Wine Bottles

We creatively repurpose wine bottles into decorations and planters, encouraging guests to do the same at home.

Reusing Food Waste

Our kitchen composts food waste, turning scraps into useful products for our garden.

Feeding Program for the Elderly and Disabled

We provide meals for the elderly and disabled in our community, making a meaningful impact.
