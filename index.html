About Us

At Sirikoi Lodge, we are committed to sustainability and community development. Our mission is to protect the environment while enriching the lives of those in our community.

Image 1 (Feeding and Beadwork Programs)

Feeding programs for Elderly and Disabled Community Members:

"By organizing feeding initiatives tailored to the needs of the disabled and elderly, we provide access to wholesome meals within our community. This not only fosters overall health and wellness but also enhances their quality of life through practical aid, fostering social interactions, and cultivating a sense of inclusion and support."

Emergency Relief Assistance:

"During times of adversity, Sirikoi stands united with our community, extending emergency aid and assistance to those facing hardship. Whether it involves distributing food, repairing roads/bridges, or providing essential resources, we are committed to offering a supportive hand in times of need."

Empowering Communities with Beadwork Artistry:

"We take immense pride in supporting local artisans and preserving traditional craftsmanship through our Sirikoi Duka/shop. Through the art of beadwork, we honor indigenous traditions, forge community bonds, and nurture an appreciation for local crafts. Talented artisans from Ngare Ndare Village, Meru, Melako, Llasamis, and Ilingwesi pour their passion and dedication into crafting exquisite pieces, ensuring the preservation of age-old techniques while embracing sustainable practices. Each beadwork creation narrates a compelling tale of skill and resilience."

Your Impact:

"By purchasing beadwork items, you directly empower these artisans and their families. Your support enables economic self-sufficiency, facilitates access to education, and enriches the overall welfare of our community."

Image 2 (School Programs)

Education Support:

"We are dedicated to shaping a brighter future through education. We invest in the next generation, equipping them with the tools necessary for success through the provision of scholarships, bursaries, and infrastructure development. Dozens of bursaries have been awarded to deserving students within our community."

Vocational Training:

"We support Pepo la Tumaini Jangwani, who specialize in vocational training for street-connected vulnerable youths by providing them with basic education and using a holistic approach to empower these children heading households."

Organic Farming Practices:

"Through regenerative agriculture, we prioritize soil health and promote practices like cover cropping, crop rotation, companion planting, natural pest control, and minimal tillage for the best year-round yields. This shift towards ecologically sound farming methods has encouraged biodiversity and the surrounding ecosystem to benefit as well. We have taken up the initiative and created shared learning experiences, through on-site workshops, hands-on learning, and school garden conversions, to create a space for intergenerational skills to be passed down."

Image 3 (Community Empowerment Programs)

Job Creation:

"We enable our community through meaningful employment opportunities, fostering growth, inclusivity, and diversity, with 74% of our staff being local residents. We take pride in cultivating an environment where everyone feels valued, supported, and empowered to thrive. This is achieved through the implementation of policies and practices that prioritize fairness, equity, empowerment, and personal growth within the workplace."

Sustainability Initiatives

Wood Ash Vinegar from Charcoal

We produce wood ash vinegar from leftover charcoal. This initiative reduces waste and provides a natural cooking and cleaning solution.

Reusing Wine Bottles

We creatively repurpose wine bottles into decorations and planters, encouraging guests to do the same at home.

Reusing Food Waste

Our kitchen composts food waste, turning scraps into useful products for our garden.

Feeding Program for the Elderly and Disabled

We provide meals for the elderly and disabled in our community, making a meaningful impact.
